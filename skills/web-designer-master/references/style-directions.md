# Style Directions

## 1. Editorial Minimal

- Mood: quiet confidence, premium, timeless
- Palette: warm neutrals + one deep accent
- Typography: high-contrast serif headline + clean sans body
- Layout: large whitespace, strong headline blocks, restrained motion

## 2. Techno Industrial

- Mood: precise, high-performance, modern
- Palette: graphite, steel, electric accent
- Typography: geometric sans + monospaced micro-labels
- Layout: modular grid with intentional asymmetry and data-like motifs

## 3. Organic Craft

- Mood: human, tactile, refined
- Palette: muted earth tones + dark ink text
- Typography: expressive humanist sans + elegant secondary serif
- Layout: soft corners, layered textures, gentle transition curves

## 4. Bold Brand Campaign

- Mood: energetic, memorable, high-impact
- Palette: 2 strong brand colors + neutral support
- Typography: condensed display face + readable body
- Layout: oversized statements, dynamic overlap, strong section contrast

## 5. Neo Luxury

- Mood: exclusive, polished, cinematic
- Palette: deep base + metallic-like highlight tones
- Typography: refined serif display + understated sans
- Layout: dramatic hero, deliberate pacing, subtle premium animation
